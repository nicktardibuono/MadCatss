Mad Cats

  • High Concept: Shoot the cats avoid there shots and then catch them.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player will be forced to decide whether or not to catch the cats based upon the other shots.
  • Genre: This game is a Shooting/Stragety game.
  • Game play: The player controls a box moving left and right. They shoot with the spacebar and try to spread the love to the cats while avoiding the hate.After spreading love they must catch the cats.
  • Prototype goal: Use differnt keyboard actions, timing, gravity and collsions.
  • Features: Game fetures three different parts which are shoot, dodge and catch.
  • Setting/Premise: You are guy named john carrying a box looking for his cats that ran away from home. When you find them they begin to hurl there hatred torwards you. Armed only with love you must shoot your cats and catch them to bring them home.
  • Story: Nothing more than what is mentioned above.
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers.
  • Play time: At the maximum a game can last 60 seconds.
  • Strategies: Be patient let the cats fall to you. Also, shooting cats multiple times will continue to reward points.
